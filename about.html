About Us

About Us

Landmark International Auto Spare Parts is a company based in the United Arab Emirates. We are celebrating our successful decade of operation in 2018. We are the leading Auto Lamps, Body Parts and Cooling Parts Importer and exporter in the Middle East and Asia.
 
Landmark employs more than 130 hard workers across multiple facilities in the country. Possessing a skilled and service oriented workforce which benefits from our assessment & training programs led by a strong leadership and manpower development team. Embedded with our organization is pure focus on Teamwork, customer service and commitment to quality are our core values. 
 With our Head Quarters in Sharjah, UAE. and our Central Warehouse facility at Al Jurf, Ajman, we are spanned across the country with a network of over 8 branches / sales outlets at Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Al Ain, Ajman and soon in Ras Al Khaima and Fujairah respectively. We reach out to customers through a network of over 2500 dealers. 
 The wide variety and vast spectrum of Spares for diverse makes and models of cars with a SKU of about 30,000 Items stored in a facility of over 200,000 Sq.ft area is the sophistication we are known for. Our Supply chain with a fleet of over 40 vehicles ensures our promises kept promptly. 
Our personalized nature and punctuality has won scores of direct export and re- export customers from countries in the Middle East and Asia. Assuring our commitment to values and excellence, we remain.

Our Vision
To mark ourselves as the most preferred, dynamic, innovative and leading organisation in auto lamps and body parts industry of Asia.

Our Mission
At Landmark we live to ensure the mission of "Walk in bearing trust and walk out wearing smile"

Our People
