About Landmark International

Landmark international is one of the largest Auto Body Parts Company in the region today, dealing in Japanese, Korean, European and American aftermarket Auto lamps and Body parts. Under the unique, prompt and effective leadership of our Founder and Managing Director Mr. Gigy Antony, Landmark has grown phenomenally over the last decade, Thanks to the wonderful team of dedicated employees for making it big....

Chairman's Message

It is our strong belief that our commitment to quality and will to succeed made Landmark our sister concern as UAE’s leading Auto Lamps & Body Parts Trading Company and subsequently start this new venture in India. It is essentially the challenges that made us walk through the path of success.

Looking forward to more bright years ahead. We renew our pledge to remain committed to excellence, keep abreast the values, changes, and innovation in the industry. By the incorporation of GG TRADING ENTERPRISE PRIVATE LIMITED, we intend to serve the Indian market for all its Auto Lamp requirements with the strength of our experience and knowledge of DEPO AUTO LAMPS.
